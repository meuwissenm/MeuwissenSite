Cooking and coding

My other passion aside from coding is cooking. I think coding and cooking have a lot of similarities. Coordinating the stovetop, the kitchen equipment, and the oven trying to figure out when I should start cooking the different parts of my meal and what resources are going to to be used or shared feels like coordinating multi-threaded processes.

When I repeat my favorite recipes I begin to understand them well enough that I can change my cooking methods to fit better with the meal I want to make and how to make each process more modular, it reminds me of the thinking process behind object oriented programming.

And I also learned cleaning up while cooking frees up resources and makes it easier to clean up later, just like garbage collection in programming.

Cooking and coding both take a lot of forethought and logic to coordinate the different moving parts and have all the result turn out well.
